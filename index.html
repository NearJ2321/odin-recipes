Sandwich

6cmX6cm

This grilled cheese is a delicious breakfast idea that should satisfy your foood desires.

The list of ingredientes for this easy grilled cheese recipe include:

  • Your fav selection of Cheese
  • Butter(optional)
  • Your fav selection of Bread

The steps for making this easy but delicious sandwich are listed below:

  1. Take your bread and cover one slice with some butter
  2. Place the bread on the grill
  3. Add that delicious cheese on top of the slioce that has butter on
  4. "Close" your sandwich and just let it on the grill until both sides are toasty and the chesse is melted
  5. Serve and enjoy!
